Features

Instant card creation

Flashcard creation as fast as sending a message to your friend - one message sent - one card created.

It's very convenient to add new cards on the go when you just heard new word and want to save it for learning

Fast and simple learning process

Use your brain to remind the other side of the card and answer with predefined buttons - no typing required.

I learn my cards in few clicks anytime when I have a minute - walking, in a queue, in a toilet.

Smart learning scheduler

Spaced Repetition algorithm together with AI schedule each your flashcard in a way that you learn it faster and retain longer.

I don't need to think what cards І should repeat and don't need to review the entire cards list every time to not forget cards - the smart scheduler does everything itself and shows only cards that you need to learn or repeat at the moment.
